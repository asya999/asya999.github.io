About me

My name is Asya Kamsky and I work with and for MongoDB.

This site's host went out of business and I'm in the process of moving all the content to github pages. Please excuse the brief interruption in service!

I've got many sites on the web that I contribute to, this is one where I'm publishing many questions and answers about MongoDB that come up on mailing lists, Google Groups, Stackoverflow, and live discussions.

If you have a question you'd like me to answer, feel free to e-mail it to asya@mongodb.com and I will try to include it. I tend to answer questions that others are also curious about, and I usually try not to duplicate the information that's already in the MongoDB docs.
